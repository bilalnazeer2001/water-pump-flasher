💧 Water Pump Controller

Flash firmware straight from this page — no laptop needed.

Your browser can't flash. Use Chrome or Edge on a computer or an Android phone (with a USB-OTG cable). Access to the serial port was blocked.
How to flash (Android phone):
  1. Connect the ESP32 to your phone with a USB-OTG cable.
  2. Open this page in Chrome.
  3. Tap Connect & Flash and pick the USB serial device.
  4. Wait for "flashing complete", then unplug.
This first flash erases everything and installs the partition layout. Afterwards, all updates are wireless via the device's WaterPump-Setup Wi-Fi (no cable). iPhones can't do USB flashing — use Android or a PC.
